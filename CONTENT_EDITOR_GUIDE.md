## ‚úèÔ∏è Common Content Updates

### Change Homepage Headline

1. **Login** to WordPress
2. Click **Site Content** in left menu
3. Click "Homepage Hero"
4. Update **Headline** field
5. Click **Update** (blue button)
6. ‚úÖ Changes go live within 1 hour (automatic rebuild)

---

### Add New Product

1. **Login** to WordPress
2. Click **Products** ‚Üí **Add New**
3. Fill in:
   - **Product Name:** e.g., "Handwoven Storage Basket"
   - **Description:** Full product story
   - **Short Description:** 2-3 sentences
   - **Regular Price:** 49.00
   - **Sale Price:** 39.00 (optional)
   - **Stock Status:** In stock
   - **Stock Quantity:** 10
4. **Upload Images:**
   - Click "Set product image" (main photo)
   - Add more photos in "Product gallery"
5. **Select Category:** Storage Baskets, Kitchen & Dining, or Wall Baskets
6. Click **Publish**
7. ‚úÖ Product appears on site after rebuild

---

### Add Artisan Story

1. **Login** to WordPress
2. Click **Artisan Stories** ‚Üí **Add Story**
3. Fill in:
   - **Title:** Artisan's name + "Story" (e.g., "Maria's Weaving Journey")
   - **Main Content:** Write the full story (2-3 paragraphs)
   - **Artisan Name:** Full name
   - **Location:** City in Kenya
   - **Primary Craft:** Select from dropdown
   - **Years of Experience:** Number
   - **Featured Quote:** Memorable quote from artisan
4. **Upload Photo:** Click "Set featured image"
5. **Link Products:** Select products this artisan makes
6. Click **Publish**
7. ‚úÖ Story appears on "Artisan Stories" page

---

### Update Collection Description

1. **Login** to WordPress
2. Click **Products** ‚Üí **Categories**
3. **Hover** over category name (e.g., "Storage Baskets")
4. Click **Edit**
5. Update **Long Description** field (scroll down)
6. Click **Update**
7. ‚úÖ Description updates on collection page

## üé® Image Guidelines

### Product Photos
- **Size:** 1200 x 1200 pixels (square)
- **Format:** JPG or PNG
- **Quality:** High resolution, well-lit
- **Background:** Clean, white or neutral
- **Show:** Product from multiple angles

### Artisan Photos
- **Size:** 800 x 600 pixels (landscape)
- **Format:** JPG
- **Quality:** High resolution
- **Content:** Artisan at work or with their creations
- **Style:** Natural, authentic

## ‚úÖ Content Checklist

Before publishing a product:
- [ ] Product name is clear and descriptive
- [ ] Price is set (regular and sale if applicable)
- [ ] At least 1 high-quality photo uploaded
- [ ] Product gallery has 3-5 photos
- [ ] Category is selected
- [ ] Stock status is "In stock"
- [ ] Stock quantity is set
- [ ] Description tells the product story
- [ ] Short description is concise

Before publishing an artisan story:
- [ ] Title includes artisan's name
- [ ] Full story is 2-3 paragraphs
- [ ] All custom fields filled out
- [ ] Featured image uploaded
- [ ] Related products linked

## üÜò Need Help?

### Something not working?
1. **Log out and back in** to WordPress
2. **Clear your browser cache:** Ctrl+Shift+R (PC) or Cmd+Shift+R (Mac)
3. **Wait 1 hour** for changes to appear
4. **Contact developer** if still not working

### Forgot your password?
1. Go to login page: https://www.gaaka.com/dev/wp-admin
2. Click "Lost your password?"
3. Enter your email
4. Check email for reset link

### Accidentally deleted something?
- WordPress saves revisions! Contact your developer to restore.
